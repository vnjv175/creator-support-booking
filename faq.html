Getting Started
How long does it take to become a successful model?
Similar to any other enterprise, the output is directly proportional to the input. This is not an instant solution — it's an opportunity to establish a business that can potentially earn you a significant fortune within a relatively brief period, but only if you put in the effort. The return on investment of your time here is exceptionally substantial. However, we can only provide you with the opportunity; it's up to you to seize it.
What do I need to bring to the table?
To work with us, you must be at least 18 years of age and completely dedicated to achieving success. You should be able to devote a minimum of three hours per day to the business. Though we prioritize your privacy, you'll need to show your face on camera, as it's what the highest-earning models do. Nevertheless, we'll take all measures to block your desired regions and protect your identity.
What kind of creators do you work with?
Collaborating with models is a significant investment for us, since we handle everything except content creation. Hence, we're genuinely seeking the cream of the crop. Additionally, we're interested in partnering with models who are committed to working with us over the long haul. This way, we can expand your business to much greater heights, and you can earn significantly more! If this resonates with you, please complete the application form.
I have zero followers and no experience. Can I still do this?
Absolutely! Many of our most successful creators started from zero. We handle marketing, fan engagement, and growth strategy — you just focus on creating content. Your work ethic and commitment matter far more than your current following.
Will this work around my schedule?
Yes! Many of our creators work this alongside school, a job, or other commitments. You set your own schedule for content creation. Our chat team handles fan engagement 24/7, so you're not tied to your phone all day. The more time you invest, the faster you'll grow — but flexibility is built in.
What if I want to stop? Am I locked into a contract?
We want creators who want to be here. While we do have agreements in place to protect both parties, we're not in the business of holding anyone hostage. If it's not working out, we'll have an honest conversation and find the best path forward.
Earnings & Payments
How much do I have to pay?
Our services are entirely free of cost. All we require is that you make us your business partner, and we receive a percentage of your earnings each time money is generated. In exchange, we expect you to be dependable, responsible, and professional.
How much money can I realistically make?
Earnings vary widely based on your commitment, content quality, and consistency. Some creators earn a few hundred dollars in their first month, while top performers make $10K–$50K+ monthly. We're transparent: this isn't a get-rich-quick scheme, but creators who put in the work consistently see life-changing income.
How and when do I get paid?
Payouts are processed weekly or bi-weekly depending on your preference. We support bank transfers and PayPal. Contact Imari to set up or change your payment method.
Content & Streaming
How many hours do I need to stream?

Referral Program
How does the referral program work?
Refer creators to Aruna and earn commission on their revenue:
  • 5% intro rate for each creator's first 3 months
  • 2.5-3% lifetime after that, based on your tier:
    • Base (2.5%): <$500K monthly revenue
    • Gold (2.75%): $500K - $2M monthly revenue
    • Diamond (3%): $2M+ monthly revenue
Apply to become a referrer and we'll send you a unique link to share.
Who can be a referrer?
Anyone can apply — current Aruna creators, appointment setters, affiliate marketers, or anyone with access to potential creators. Apply here. Once approved, you can access your personalized referral links anytime.
When do I get paid for referrals?
Referral commissions are paid monthly. Once your referred creator starts earning, you'll see commissions in your next payout cycle.
How do I view my referral earnings and get my links?
Once approved as a referrer, you'll receive a unique access code. Use it to:
  • View your dashboard — track all your referrals, monthly revenue, tier status, and total earnings
  • Get your referral links — generate personalized links to share with potential creators
