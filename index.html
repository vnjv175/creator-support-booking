Common Questions

Referral Program FAQ

How are commissions calculated and verified?

Commissions are calculated as a percentage of your referred creator's gross monthly revenue (before any splits). We use OnlyFans' built-in analytics and FansMetric for verification. You can see real-time earnings in your referrer dashboard.

When and how do I get paid?

Commissions are paid monthly, within the first 10 business days of each month for the previous month's earnings. We pay via bank transfer (ACH/wire) or PayPal — your choice. No minimum payout threshold.

What happens if my referral leaves Aruna?

You earn commissions for as long as your referred creator is active with Aruna. If they leave and later return, your referral credit is still attached — you'll start earning again when they reactivate.

Can I refer creators who are already with another agency?

Yes. Many of our highest-earning referrals come from creators switching agencies. As long as they sign with Aruna through your referral link or direct introduction, you get credit. We handle the transition process.

Is there a minimum payout threshold?

No. We pay every dollar you earn, every month. No minimums, no holdbacks, no hidden fees.

How do I track my referrals and earnings?

Every referrer gets access to a personal dashboard showing active referrals, monthly revenue, commission earnings, and payout history. You'll also receive monthly summary emails with detailed breakdowns.
